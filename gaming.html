Is gaming benficial to society?

Many people have the misguided belief that video games lead to a rotting brain, but is this the case? In fact, many studies have concluded that video games actually benefit our cognition. They can help with many skills in life including, but not limited to:

  • Problem solving
  • Spatial logic
  • Reaction time
  • Hand eye coordination
  • Strategic thinking
Depending on the games you play, different skills can be improved.
